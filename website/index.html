Distraction Free Instagram

Get your focus back.

Instagram is a great way to keep in contact with friends and family, but it has many features that are designed to be addictive and to make you use the app far more than you wanted to.

With Distraction Free Instagram, you can choose disable the features that you find the most distracting. You can choose to disable the feed, explore page, reels or shopping page with the flick of a switch, and you can also lock in your preferences to make sure you don't fall back on old habits.

Download

This app is currently only available for android users. Make sure to uninstall instagram first, and then install DFinstagram by opening your file explorer and tapping on the downloaded file.
